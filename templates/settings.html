how to set up ntfy (2 minutes)
  1. Install the ntfy app on your phone (iOS / Android)
  2. Open the app and tap + to subscribe to a topic
  3. Pick a unique name (e.g. something-random) — this is your topic
  4. Enter that same name in the ntfy topic field below
  5. Save settings — you'll start getting alerts!
